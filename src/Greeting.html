Greeting to YouWebIt Sample Website. This website is launch when YouWebIt does not find any website files.

To use YouWebIt, just copy YouWebIt.exe in your WebSite Directory.

Use "Save config" for configuration option.

How can it be usefull? :

  • Send your website to your mother. Package your website & YouWebIt in a zip file. Share it. You just have share your first website desktop application.
  • Do you want to show your work? : Just run YouWebIt.exe without having to configure IIS, or to open Visual Studio / Blend...
  • Have you say crossbrowser development? : Run your Website quickly in all installed browser (IE, FireFox, MultipleIEs are autodetected).
